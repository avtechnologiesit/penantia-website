The mechanism

Why delivery form
changes everything.

01
Ionic mineral state
Minerals in ionic form carry an electrical charge — the gut wall actively transports them. Mineral oxides and carbonates absorb at 4–22% at best. Ionic minerals skip the conversion step where most supplements are lost.
02
Fulvic acid as carrier
Fulvic acid chelates ionic minerals and actively transports them across the intestinal membrane — a fundamentally different pathway that bypasses the bottlenecks where most supplements are lost.
03
Resin form only
Capsule and powder equivalents introduce a dissolution step that degrades active compounds. Resin delivers everything in solution — zero degradation between the jar and the gut wall.
04
Mitochondrial ATP support
Peer-reviewed studies (PMID 23439107, 30850811) demonstrate purified Shilajit increases mitochondrial ATP output — not by stimulating the body, but supplying the mineral substrate the cell needs to produce energy properly.
