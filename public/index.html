The mechanism

Why delivery form changes everything.

01
Ionic mineral state
Minerals in ionic form carry an electrical charge, allowing the gut wall to transport them actively. Mineral oxides absorb at 4–22%. Ionic minerals skip the conversion step entirely.
02
Fulvic acid as the carrier
Fulvic acid chelates ionic minerals and actively transports them across the intestinal membrane — a fundamentally different absorption pathway that bypasses the bottlenecks where most supplements are lost.
03
Resin form only
Capsule and powder forms introduce a dissolution step that degrades active compounds. Resin delivers everything in solution — the form the body encounters it directly — with zero degradation.
04
Mitochondrial ATP support
Clinical studies (PMID 23439107, 30850811) demonstrate purified Shilajit increases mitochondrial ATP output by supplying the mineral substrate the cell requires to produce energy at the source.
